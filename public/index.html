Nodebook is a tool to explore the treasure trove of your Evernote data. It is meant to help you visually wander around your notes and discover new connections.

  1. To start, choose "Load Evernote data" from the system bar ☝️
  2. You can save the state of your graph
  3. If your graph is messy, try hiding individual nodes or whole notebooks ( from "Notebooks" menu in the system bar)
